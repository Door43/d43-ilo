===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Recall the marvelous things**  - AT: "Remember the wonderful things"
  * **from his mouth**  - AT: "that he spoke" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you descendants of Israel his servant, you people of Jacob, his chosen ones**  - These phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **His decrees are on all the earth**  - AT: "His laws are for all the people of the earth"
