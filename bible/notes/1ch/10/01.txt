===== Translation =====

    * **Every man of Israel** - AT: "Many fighting men of Israel" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
    * **fell down dead** - "died"
    * **Mount Gilboa** - This is the name of a mountain.  (See: [[ilo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Abinadab ... Malchishua** - These are names of men.
