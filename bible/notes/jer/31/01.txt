===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At that time** - This refers to the time when God punishes the wicked.
  * **this is Yahweh’s declaration** - See how you translated this in [[ilo:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]].
   * **Yahweh appeared to me** - Here "me" refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
