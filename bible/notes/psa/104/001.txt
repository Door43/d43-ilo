===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[ilo:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]].
This psalm is a song of praise.

  * **splendor and majesty** - These two words have similar meanings and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **You cover yourself with light as with a garment** - AT: "You are covedred in light" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **You lay the beams of your chambers on the clouds** - AT: "You lay the structures of your bedroom in heaven" (See: [[ilo:ta:workbench:translate:anthro]])
  * **chambers** - "upper room" 
  * **you make the clouds your chariot** - AT: "you ride the clouds like a chariot" (See: [[ilo:ta:workbench:translate:anthro]])
  * **you walk on the wings of the wind** - AT: "you walk upon the wind" (See: [[ilo:ta:workbench:translate:anthro]])
