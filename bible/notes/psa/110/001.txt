===== Translation Notes: =====

This psalm is a song of praise to the king of Israel.\\ 
See: [[ilo:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of David or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **Sit at my right hand **- AT: "Sit at the place of honor which I have for you." (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **make your enemies your footstool **- AT: "put your enemies under your power." (See:[[:ilo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
