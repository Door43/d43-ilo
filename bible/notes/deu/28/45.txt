===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All these curses will come on you and will pursue and overtake you until you are destroyed** - Moses describes the curses as a person chasing and conquering the people. AT: "Yahweh will cause all these curses to happen to you until he destroys you" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to the voice of Yahweh your God** - AT: "to what Yahweh your God said" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **his commandments and his statutes** - These words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. AT: "all of his commandments" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
