===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Morashtite** - This means he is from Moresheth which is  a town in Judah. (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **in the days of Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah** - "when Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah were kings of Judah"
  * **the word that he saw concerning Samaria and Jerusalem** - AT: "the vision that he saw concerning the people of Samaria and Jerusalem" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
