===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his heart had turned away from him** - That is, "Solomon's heart had turned away from Yahweh." AT: "Solomon stopped worshiping Yahweh" (See: [[:ilo:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he had appeared to him twice** - That is, "Yahweh had appeared to Solomon twice."
