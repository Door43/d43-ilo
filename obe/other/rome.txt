====== Rome, Roman ======


===== Facts: =====

Ti agdinama nga aldaw, Rome ti kapital ti nassion a Italy.

  * Idi panawen ni Jesus ken dagiti adalanna. Rome ti ili ti Roman Empire
  * Ti Roman Empire iti mangituray dagiti amin rehiyon ti aglawlaw ti Mediterranean a Baybay, agraman Israel.
  * The term, "Roman" refers to anything relating to the regions that the government in Rome controlled, including Roman citizens and Roman officials.
  * Ti sao a "Roman" ket maipapan kadagiti amin a rehiyon agraman gobyerno ni Rome a mangituray agraman dagiti Roman a tatao ken adda turayna.
  * The Apostle Paul was taken to the city of Rome as a prisoner because he preached the good news about Jesus.
