====== Egypt, Egyptian ======

===== Dagiti Kinapudno: =====

Egypt is a country in the northeast part of Africa, to the south and west of the land of Canaan.

Maysa.

  * Ti taga Egypt ket dagiti naiyanak idjayen ken ti nagnaeden dagiti kapuunan da sadjay.
  * Idi punganay, maysa a mannakabalin ken nabaknang a nasion ti Egypt.
  * Ancient Egypt was divided into two parts, Lower and Upper. "Lower Egypt" was where the Nile River flowed down into the Mediterranean Sea.
  * Idi punganay nagudwa ti Egypt ti dua, ti Ngato ken ti Baba. Idjay Baba ket isu ti Karayan Nilo agayos pababa idjay Baybay ti Mediterranean.
  * Idjay Egypt napan ni Joseph ken Maria kadwa da ti ubing pay idi a Jesus tapno libasan da ni Herod.
  * Ti mano a daras idi bassiten ti makan idjay Canaan, mapan idjay Egypt dagiti kaamaan ti Israel tapno gumatang ti taraon a para ti pamilya da.
