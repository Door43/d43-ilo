====== Pharaoh, king of Egypt ======

===== Dagiti Kinapudno: =====

In ancient times, the kings who ruled over the country of Egypt were called pharaohs.

Idi punganay, dagiti ar-ari a nangituray iti nasion nga Egypt ket maawagan ti pharaohs.

  * Altogether, over 300 pharaohs ruled Egypt for more than 2,000 years.
  * Ummabot to tallo gasut nga pharaoh ti nangituray ti Egypt ti agarup dua ribo a tawtawen.
  * These Egyptians kings were very powerful and wealthy.
  * Dagitoy nga ar-ari nga Egyptians ket mannakabalin ken nabaknang unay.
  * Several of these pharaohs are mentioned in the Bible.
  * Dagiti sumagmano kadagitoy nga pharaohs ket nadakamat diay Bibliya.
  * Often this title is used as a name rather than as a title. In these cases, it is capitalized as "Pharaoh."
  * Dagitoy a titulo ket kanayon a maar-aramat nga nagan kaysa titulo, maipadakkel daytoy a kas "Pharaoh."
