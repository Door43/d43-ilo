====== Anak ti Dios, ti Anak ======

===== Dagiti Kinapudno: =====

Ni Jesus ti Anak ti Dios, a immay dituy lubong kas maysa tao tapno isalakan ken agari dituy lubong.

  * Ti Anak ti Dios ket agpada kas Dios Ama, ken naan-anay a Dios.
  * Ti Dios Ama, Dios Anak, ken Dios Ispiritu Santu ket maymaysa ti kaipapananna.
  * Saan a kas tao a anak, ti Anak ti Dios kanayun nga adda.
  * Idi un-unana, ti Anak ti Dios ket addan kabayatan a parsuwaen ti lubong, naitipon wennu naikaddwa iti Ama ken ti Santu Ispiritu.
  * Gapu ni Jesus ket Anak ti Dios, ayaten ken pagtultulnogan na ti Amana,ken ti Amana ay-ayatenna met.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * For the term, "Son of God," it is best to translate "Son" with the same word the language would naturally use to refer to a "son" of a human father.
  * Make sure the word used to translate "son" fits with the word used to translate "father" and that these words are the most natural ones to express a true father-son relationship.
