====== naimbag damag,  ======

===== Dagiti Kaipapanan: =====

Ti sao a " naimbag a damag" kayat na sawen ti mensahe wennu pakaammu a ibagana kadagiti tattao a pagimbagan ken pakaragsakanda.

  * Ti Biblia, masansan a maiturong ti mensahe maipanggep iti panangisalakan ti Dios para kadagiti tatao babaen kenni Jesus a sakripisiyo iddiay krus.
  * Adda met sabali a pannakapaitarus ti "naimbag a damag" kas kuma "naibag a damag ni Jesus" wennu "naimbag damag ti Dios" ken "naimbag a damag ti pagaryan."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Different ways to translate this term could include, "good message" or "good announcement" or "glad word" or "God's message of salvation" or "the good things God teaches about Jesus."
  * Depending on the context, ways to translate the phrase, "good news of" could include, "good news/message about" or "good message from" or "the good thing God tells us about" or "what God says about how he saves people."

(See: [[:ilo:obe:kt:salvation]], [[:ilo:obe:other:sacrifice]], [[:ilo:obe:other:kingdom]])
