====== panangtungpal ======

===== Kaipapanan: =====

Ti sao nga"panangtungpal"ket addaan kaipapanan a mangpunno wennu mangleppas ti maysa a banag a naipadtu,pinanggep,naibilin wennu ninamnama.

  * Idi a ti padtu ket natungpal,kayat a saritaen nga ti Dios tinungpal na ti naipadtu.
  * Nu ti maysa a tao tinungpal na ti maysa a kari,kayat a saritaen a tinungpal na ti anyaman a naisawang na.
  * Ti panangtungpal ti maysa a pagrebbengan ket kayat na a saritaen a panangaramid ti naidutok wennu naitalek kenka.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, "fulfill" could be translated as "accomplish" or "complete" or "cause to happen" or "obey" or "perform."
  * The phrase "has been fulfilled" could also be translated as, "has come true" or "has happened" or "has taken place."
  * Ways to translate "fulfill" as in "fulfill your ministry" could include, "complete" or "perform" or "practice" or "serve other people as God has called you to do."
