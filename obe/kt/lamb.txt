====== lamb, Lamb of God  ======


===== Kaipapanan: =====

Urbon ti nagan ti ubbing nga karnero. Ti karnero ket addaan uppat nga saka na ken napuskol nga dutdot na.​

  * Ti karnero ket awan panglaban na ken nalaka nga maiyawawawan. Inyasping ti Dios dagiti tattao iti karnero.
  * Binilin ti Dios dagiti tattao nga agidaton ti awan pakapilawan na nga karnero ken dagiti urbon kenkuana.
  * Naawagan ni Jesus nga "Urbon a Karnero ti Dios" nga naidaton para ti basbasol dagiti tattao. Awan pagkurangan ken pakapilawan na nga daton gapu ta awanan basol.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * If sheep are known in the language area, the name for their young should be used to translate the terms "lamb" and "Lamb of God."
  * "Lamb of God" could be translated as "God's (sacrificial) Lamb," or "Lamb sacrificed to God" or "(sacrificial) Lamb from God."
  * If sheep are not known, this term could be translated as "the young of a sheep" with a footnote that describes what sheep are like. The note could also compare sheep and lambs to an animal from that area that lives in herds, is timid and defenseless, and which often wanders off.
  * Also consider how this term is translated in a Bible translation of a nearby local or national language. (See: [[ilo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown|How to Translate Unknowns]])
