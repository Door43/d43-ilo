====== worship ======


===== Kaipapanan: =====

"Nu agrukbab" ket isu ti panangraem, panagdaydayaw wenno panagtulnog kangrunaan kenni Apo Dios.

  * Nu narabaw nga pagsasao daytoy ket " panagdumog" wenno " pangibabam ti bagim" tapno laeng padayawan ti siasinoman.
  * Pagrukbaban tayo ti Dios nu pagserbian ken pagraeman tayo isu na, babaen ti panagdaydayaw ken panagtulnog kenkuana.
  * Nu agrukbab dagiti taga Israel ti Dios, pasaray agidaton da ti animal idiay altar.
  * Adda dagiti tattao nga agrukbab kadagiti didiosen.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "worship" could be translated as "bow down to" or "honor and serve" or "honor and obey."
  * In some contexts, it could also be translated as "humbly praise" or "give honor and praise."
 
(See also: [[ilo:obe:other:sacrifice]], [[ilo:obe:other:praise]], [[:ilo:obe:other:honor]])
