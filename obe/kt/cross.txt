====== krus ======



**Dagiti Kaipapanan:**

Ti krus ket maysa a kayu a naipatakder manipud daga, nga adda baratilanna a nakalansa iti asider ti murdong.

  * Idi panawen ti Roman Empire, ti gobyerno dagiti Roman ket igalgalu ken ilanlansa dagiti criminal iti ingga a matay saddiay.
  * Ni Jesus ket napabasul iti maysaa basol a basol a saanna met nga inaramid, ket dagiti Romans ket inlansa iddiay agingga a natay.


===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could be translated using a term in the target language that refers to the shape of a cross.
  * Consider describing the cross as the thing on which people were killed, using phrases such as "execution post" or "tree of death."
  * Also consider how this word is translated in a Bible translation in a local or national language. (See: [[ilo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown|How to Translate Unknowns]])
