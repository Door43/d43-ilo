====== agpayso, kina-agpayso ======

===== Kaipapanan: =====

Ti "agpayso" ken "kinaagpasyo" itudo na ti pannakapaawat kadagiti banbannag a pudno, paspasamak a napasamaken, ken sarsarita nga naibagan.

  * Dagiti agpayso a banag ket pudno, nainkalintegan, nalinteg, ken nakabasar ti kinapudno.
  * Ti kinaagpayso ket pannakaawat, pammati, banag a pudno, wenno sarsarita nga agpayso.
  * Inpakita ni Jesus ti kinaagpayso ti Dios kadagiti insao na.
  * Ti sasao ti Dios ket kinaagpayso. Ibaga na dagiti banbanag nga pudno a napasamak ken isuro na dagiti agpayso maipapan ti Dios ken amin nga inaramid na.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context and what is being described, the term "true" could be translated by "real" or "factual" or "correct" or "right" or "certain" or "genuine."
  * Ways to translated the term "truth" could include, "what is true" or "fact" or "certainty" or "principle."
  * Tips for translating these phrases:
    * "accept the truth" --- "believe what is true (about God)"
    * "I tell you the truth" --- "This true thing I'm about to say is very important:"
    * "learn the truth" -- "learn true things that God has taught us."
