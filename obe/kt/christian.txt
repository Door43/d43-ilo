====== Kristiano, wennu pasurot ni Kristo (Christ) ======



**Dagiti Kaipapapnan:**

Sakbay a nagsubli ni Jesus sadddiay langit dagiti tattao ket nangrugida a awagan dagiti pasurot na a “Kristiano” isu’t gapu na a maawaganda a pasurot ni Kristo (Christ).

  * Ti Kristiano ket tao a sumursurot ken Jesus. A mamati ken Jesus a isu’t anak ti Dios, ken adda talek na ken Jesus a mangisakan kenkuana kadagiti basbasolna.
  * Iti agdinama a panawen masansan a ti sao a “Kristian” ket maar-aramat kadagiti siasino man a mamati iti linteg, Ngem saan met a pudno a pasurot ni Jesus. Saan a daytoy ti pudno a kayat a sauen ti Kristian iti Biblia.
  * Gapu ta ti sao a “Kristian’ ti Biblia ket iturong ti maysa a pudno mamati ken Jesus, Ti Kristian ket maaw-awagan a namati,


===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could be translated as "Christ-follower" or "follower of Christ" or perhaps something like, "Christ-person."
  * Make sure that the translation of this term is distinct from terms used for disciple or apostle.
  * Be careful to translate this term with a word that can refer to everyone who believes in Jesus, not just certain groups.
  * Also consider how this term is translated in a Bible translation in a local or national language. (See: [[ilo:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown|How to Translate Unknowns]])
