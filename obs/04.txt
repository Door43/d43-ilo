====== 4. Ti Tulag Ti Dios Kenni Abraham ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?direct&}}

Many years after the flood, there were again many people in the world, and they all spoke the same language. But the people did not obey God. Instead of having children and filling the earth, they gathered together and built a city.

Adu nga tawen iti naglabas kalpasan ti dakkel nga layus ket immadu manen dagiti tao iti lubong ket maymaysa idi ti pagsasao da. Ngem imbes nga tungpalen da ti bilin ti Dios nga paadduen da ti tao ti lubong, nag maymaysa da ketdi nga nangrugi nga agipatakder ti maysa nga siyudad.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg?direct&}}

They were very proud, and they did not care about what God said. They even began building a tall tower to reach heaven. God saw that if they all kept working together, they could do many evil things.

Nakapalpalangguad da ken madi da inkaskaso ti imbagan ti Dios. Nangrugi da payen a nagpatakder ti maysa a nakangatngato a torre tapno madanun da ti langit. Nakita ti Dios a nu itultuloy da nga agmaymaysa da nga agaramid ti dakes ket mabalin da pay a makaaramid ti ad adu nga dakes nga banbanag.

''{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg?direct&}}''

''So He changed their language into many different languages and spread the people out all over the world. The city they had begun to build was called Babel, which means “confused.” ''

Isua binaliwan ti Dios iti pagsasao da iti adu a nadumaduma a pagsasao ken inwaras na dagiti tattao iti intero a lubong. Nagrugi da nga ipatakder ti siudad a maag-awagan ti Babel a kayat na a saoen ket “naallilaw”.

''{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg?direct&}}''

''Hundreds of years later, God spoke to a man named Abram. God told him, “Leave your country and family and go to the land I will show you.” ''

Ginasgasut a tawen iti napalpalabas ket nagsao ti Dios iti tao nga agnagan ti Abram. Kinuna ti Dios kenkuana, “Panawam ti lugar ken ti pamilyam ket mapan ka iti daga nga ipakitakto kenka. Bendisyonan ka ket aramiden ka a nabileg a nasyon. Aramidek nga natan-ok ti nagan mo. Bendisyonak dagiti mangbendisyon kenka ken ilunod ko dagiti agilunod kenka. Amin nga pamilya iti lubong ket mabindisyonan da gapo kenka.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg?direct&}}

So Abram obeyed. He took his wife, Sarai, and all his servants and herds of animals and went to the land God showed him, the land of Canaan. Abram lived there for many years. There God made a covenant with Abram. A covenant is an agreement between two parties.

Isua nagtulnog ni Abram iti Dios. Intugot na ti asawa na a ni Sarai agraman dagiti amin nga adipen na ken ti sanikua na ket napan da iti daga nga impakita ti Dios kenkuana isu iti daga a Canaan.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?direct&}}

God said, “Look all around you. All the land that you can see I will give to you and your descendants as an inheritance. I will bless you and make your name great.”

Idi simmamngpet da Abram idiay Canaan ket kinuna ti Dios, “Kitaem iti aglawlaw. Ited ko kenka ken ti kaputotam amin a daga a makitkitam a kas tawid yo.” Ket nagnaed da Abram iti daga a Canaan.

''{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?direct&}}''

''“I will make your descendants into a large nation, and I will be your God. I will bless those who bless you and curse those who curse you. All the people groups of the world will be blessed through you.” God also told Abram to circumcise all the men in his household as a sign of the covenant. Abram obeyed. ''

Iti maysa nga aldaw ket nagsabat da Abram ken Melchizedek a padi ti kangatoan a Dios. Binendisyonan ni Melchizedek ni Abram a kinuna na, “Bendisyonan naka koma ti Dios ko a kangatoan ken makinbagi ti langit ken daga.” Ket inted ni Abram kenni Melchizedek ti apagkapullo ti amin a sanikuana.'' ''

''{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg?direct&}}''

Many years went by, but Abram and Sarai still did not have a son. God spoke to Abram and promised again that he would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. Abram believed God's promise. God declared that Abram was righteous because he believed in God's promise.'' ''

Ngem idi naglabas ti adu a tawen keta wan latta ti anak da Abram ken Sarai. Nagsao ti Dios kenni Abram ket inkari na manen a maaddaan isuna ti putot a lalaki ken kaputotan a kas kinaadu ti bibituin iti tangatang. Namati ni Abram iti kari ti Dios. Ket inbaga ti Dios a nalinteg ni Abram gapo ta namati isuna iti kari ti Dios.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?direct&}}

Then God made a covenant with Abram. A covenant is an agreement between two parties. God said, “I will give you a son from your own body. I give the land of Canaan to your descendants.” But Abram still did not have a son.

''Maysa nga aldaw, nakita ni Abraham ni Melquizedek. Maysa isuna nga padi ti Dios nga mannakabalin iti amin. Benidisionan ni Melquizedek ni Abraham. Kinunana, b'' endisionan naka ti Dios nga mannakabalin iti amin nga akin bagi ti langit ken daga. '' Inted ni Abram ken Melquizedek iti ika-sangapulo iti parti ti kinabaknang na.''

Ket nakitulag ti Dios kenni Abram. Ti katulagan ket maysa a nagnunumoan iti dua a partidos. Kinuna ti Dios, “Ikkan ka ti maysa nga anak a lalaki nga aggapu mismo iti bagim. Ited ko met ti daga ti Canaan kadagiti kaputotam.” Ngem awan pay anak ni Abram a lalaki.
