====== 5. The Son of Promise ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-01.jpg?direct&}}

Abram and Sarai waited over twenty years, but they still had no son. God spoke to Abram and promised again that he would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. Abram believed God’s promise.

Kalpasan ti sangapulo nga tawen ngem ni Abraham ken ni sara, awan pay lang anak da.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-02.jpg?direct&}}

Then Abram’s wife said to him, “Since the LORD has not allowed me to have children and now I am too old to have children, here is my servant, Hagar. Marry her also so she can have a child for me.”

So Abram married Hagar. Hagar had a baby boy, and Abram named him Ishmael. Abram wanted God to bless Ishmael. God said, “I will bless him, but Ishmael is not the son of promise. I will not make my covenant with him.”

Isu nga pinakasaran ni Abraham ni Agar. Lalaki ti nagbalin nga anak da.Ismael ti inpanagan da ti ubing.ngem nagimon ni sara ken Agar.Idi sangapulo ket tallo ti tawen na ni Ismael,kinasarita manen ti Dios ni Abraham.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?direct&}}

“Your wife, Sarai, will have a son—he will be the son of promise. Name him Isaac. My covenant will be with him, and he will become a great nation.” Then God changed Abram’s name to Abraham, which means “father of many.” God also changed Sarai’s name to Sarah, which means “princess.”

Kinuna ti Dios ken Abraham, siak ti Dios nga mannakabalin.nagkariak kadakayo nga saan nga mapukaw ti karik kenka. Isu nga nagparintumeng ni Abraham. Kinuna ti Dios kenkuana,''Abraham, agbalin ka nga ama ti adu nga nasion.Ited ko kadagiti Appom ti lugar ti kanaan. Siak to met ti amammu da a Dios ingana.''

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-05.jpg?direct&}}

Finally, when Abraham was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham’s son. They named him Isaac as God had told them to do.

Kalpasan ti maysa nga tawen,nagpasngayen ni Sara .Isaac iti inpanagan da ti anak da kapada ti inbaga ti Dios kadakuada. Iti dayta nga panawen maysa nga gasut nan ni Abraham ket ni met Sara siam apulo ti tawen na.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-06.jpg?direct&}}

When Isaac was a young man, God tested Abraham’s faith by saying, “Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a sacrifice to me.” Again Abraham obeyed what God told him to do.

Idin dakkelen ni Isaac sinubok ti Dios ti pammati ni Abraham kanniana .kinuna ,''Ti panagdayaw mo kanniak ikuyog mo ni Isaac nga ipatpateg mo nga anak ket idaton mo.Nagtungpal manen ni Abraham ti bilin ti Dios.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-07.jpg?direct&}}

He tied up his son Isaac and laid him on an altar. He was about to kill his son when God said, “Stop! Do not hurt the boy! Now I know that you fear me because you did not keep your only son from me.”

Idinto ta magmagnada ni Abraham ken Isaac nga mapan idiay lugar nga pangidatonanna, kinuna ni Isaac,''addan ti kayo nga para iti pagdaton.ngem awan pay iti idaton tayo,''simmungbat ni Abraham,''Anak ko ti Dios iti mangted ti idaton ta.

Nearby Abraham saw a ram which was stuck in a bush. God had provided the ram to be the sacrifice instead of Isaac. Abraham happily offered the ram as a sacrifice.

Nakita ni Abraham ti maysa nga lalaki nga karnero nga nakasab-it ti sara na iti sanga ti kayo nga ababa. Dayta ti inted ti Dios nga idaton da. Idin ta idatdaton dan ti karnero maragragsakan ni Abraham.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-09.jpg?direct&}}

Then God said to Abraham, “Because you were willing to give me everything, even your only son, I promise to bless you. Your descendants will be more than the stars in the sky. Because you have obeyed me, all the families of the world will be blessed through your family.”

Kalpasan na kinuna ti Dios ken Abraham, ''Gapu ta nakasagana ka nga mangted kaniak ti amin. Pati ti anak mo nga lalaki, ikarek kenka bindisionan ka ken kadagiti appom.agbalin da nga kas kaadu iti bituen idiay langit ken gapu ta tinungpal nak,Ti amin nga pamilyam ditoy lubong.bindisionak babaen ti kaputotam''

//A Bible story from: Genesis 16-22//
