====== 22. Ti Pannakaiyanak Ni John ======

===== Tan-aw a Panagkita: =====

A priest named Zechariah was very old and had no children. An angel from God told him that he would have a son, John, who would prepare the way for the Messiah. The angel also told a young virgin named Mary that she would have a son. Maryâ€™s child would be the Messiah, the Son of the Most High God.

Ti padi a managan Zechariah ket lakayen ken awan pay putot na. Nagparang ti maysa nga anghel ti Dios a kinunana kenkauna a maaddaan to ti anak managan John, a mangisagana ti dalan para iti Messiah. Ti anghel nagparang met iti maysa a birhen agnagan Mary a mangipasngayto ti anak a lalaki. Ti anak ni Mary ket agbalinto a Messiah, Anak ti kangattuan a Dios.
