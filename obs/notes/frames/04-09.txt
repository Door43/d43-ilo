===== Katulagan Ti Dios [04-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Ket nakitulag ti Dios kenni Abram. Ti katulagan ket maysa a nagnunumoan iti dua a partidos. Kinuna ti Dios, “Ikkan ka ti maysa nga anak a lalaki nga aggapu mismo iti bagim. Ited ko met ti daga ti Canaan kadagiti kapututam.” Ngem ni Abram keta wan pay putut na a lalaki.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **two parties**  - The parties could be two people, two groups of people, or a person and a group of people. In this case the agreement was between God and Abram.
  * **from your own body**  - With his own body Abram would cause his wife to become pregnant, so that together they would have their own, natural son. This was an amazing promise, since Abram and Sarai were very old.
  * **did not have a son**  - Abram still did not have any descendants who could possess the land.
  * Ti naggapuan iti Istorya ket mabalin a adda pagsasabaliyan da iti dadduma a balikas ti Biblia.
