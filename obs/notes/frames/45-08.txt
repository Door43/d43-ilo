===== Dagiti Ethiopian Binasa Dani Isaiah [45-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Idin immasideg ni Philip iti karwahe ket nangngegna  ti taga Ethiopia nga agbasbabsa manipud iti naisuarat ni mammadtu nga Isaiah. Ti basbasaen ti lalaki ket ti paset a kunana, “Inturong da isuna  a kas karnero tapnun mapapatay ken kas karnero a naulimek ket saan a nagsasao. Trinatoda isuna a saan a nasayaat ken saanda a dinayaw isuna. Innala da ti biag na manipud kenkuana.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ethiopian** - That is, a person from the country of Ethiopia. See note on **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:45-07|[45-07]]]**.
  * **approached the chariot** - That is, "got close to the chariot" or, "came near to the chariot."
  * **as a lamb is silent** - This could also be translated as, "like a lamb that is silent even when it is about to be killed."
