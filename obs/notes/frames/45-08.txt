===== Binasa Diay Taga Ethiopian Ti Insurat Ni Isaiah [45-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Napan ni Saul idiay Jerusalem tapno kitaen na dagiti adalan sadiay ngem nagbuteng da kenkuana. Nakaapan na ni Saul kadagiti Apostoles babaen iti maysa a namati nga agnagan iti Barnabas. Imbaga na pay kadakuada nu kasano a situtured a nangaskasaba ni Saul idiay Damascus. Kalpasan daytoy, inawaten dagiti adalan ni Saul.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ethiopian**  - That is, a person from the country of Ethiopia. See note on **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:45-07|[45-07]]]**.
  * **approached the chariot**  - That is, "got close to the chariot" or, "came near to the chariot."
  * **as a lamb is silent**  - This could also be translated as, "like a lamb that is silent even when it is about to be killed."
