===== Inrabrabakda Ni Jesus [39-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Simmungbat dagiti amin a panguloen a Judio iti kangatoan a padi ket kinunada, “Maiparbeng a matay isuna!” Ket inabbungan da dagiti mata ni Jesus ket tinuptupraan da ken binaotbaot da ken inuy-uyaw da isuna.

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus, Jesus Christ, Christ Jesus]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:mock|mock, ridicule, scoff at]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **they blindfolded Jesus**  - That is. "they covered Jesus' eyes so that he could not see."
  * **spit on him**  - This could also be translated as, "spit on him to insult him" or, "spit on him in order to say that he was worthless." This was a way of showing contempt for someone.
