===== Ti Pannakitulag Ni Judas [38-02] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Kadagiti adalan ni Jesus ket maysa nga agnagan ti Judas. Ni Judas iti makin-iggem iti pagyanan ti kwarta dagiti adalan. Ngem ayayaten na ti kuwarta ket kanayun a taktakawen ti kuarta idiay suput/bag. Kalpasan a nakadanun ni Jesus ken dagiti adalan  idiay Jerusalem ket napan ni Judas kadagidiay panguluen a Judio ket inyawisna ti panangliput kadakuada ken Jesus a ti kasukat ket kwarta. Ammo Judas a ilibak dagiti panguluen a Judio a ni Jesus isu ti Mesias ken ammo na agpangpanggep da a mampapatay kenni Jesus.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in charge of the apostles' moneybag** - That is, "responsible to hold the apostles' money" or, "responsible to hold the bag that had the disciples' money in it and to distribute funds from it."
  * **loved money** - That is, "highly valued money" or, "wanted money." Some languages would not use the same word that can be used for "loved people."
