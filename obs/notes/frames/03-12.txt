===== Namaga A Daga [03-12] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Nagpalabas manen ti maysa a domingo ni Noah ket nagpatayab manen ti kalapati. Itattan, saanen a nagsubli ti kalapati. Isu immay iti panunot ni Noah nga addan siguron iti bimmabaan ti kalapati. Iti kayat na a saoen ket mabiiten nga agmaga ti daga.

===== Important Terms: =====


  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:noah]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **waited another week** - You could say, "waited seven more days." The word "waited" shows that Noah was allowing time for the floodwaters to go down before sending out the dove again.
