===== Sinarungkaran Dagiti Agpaspastor Ni Jesus [23-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Iti saan a mabayag ket nakadanon dagiti pastor iti ayan ni Jesus ken nasarakan da isuna a nakaidda iti maysa a kulluong ket kas iti imbaga ti anghel kadakuada. Maragsakan da launay ken naragsakan met uray ni Mary. Nagsubli dagiti pastor iti ayan dagiti karnero da ket dinaydayaw da ti Dios gapo kadagiti amin a nangngeg ken nakita da.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all they had heard and seen** - That is, "for everything they had heard and seen." This included the glorious angels and their amazing message, as well as seeing the newborn Messiah himself.
