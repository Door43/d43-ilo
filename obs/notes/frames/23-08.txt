===== Binisita Dagiti Agpaspastor Ni Jesus [23-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Iti saan a mabayag nakadanun ngarud dagiti pastor iti ayan ni Jesus ken nasarakan da ti ubing a nakaidda iti kulluong,kas ti imbaga dagiti anghel kadakwada. Ket naragsakan da launay. Nagsubli dagiti pastor iti ayan dagiti karnero da ket dinaydayaw da ti Dios kadagiti amin a nangngeg da ken nakita da.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all they had heard and seen** - That is, "for everything they had heard and seen." This included the glorious angels and their amazing message, as well as seeing the newborn Messiah himself.
