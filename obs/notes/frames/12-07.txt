===== Pinagadwa Ni Moses Ti Danum Ti Baybay [12-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Imbaga ti Dios kenni Moses nga ingato ken iturong na ti ima na idiay baybay tapno agbisngay ti danum. Ket induron ti Dios ti danum babaen iti angin iti makannawan ken makannigid tapno mapaadda ti dalan iti nagbisngayan ti baybay.

===== Dagiti Napapateg A Balikas: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:god|God ]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:moses|Moses]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita : =====

  * **raise his hand over the sea**  - This could be translated as, "held out his hand over the sea". This was a gesture to show that God was doing this miracle through Moses.
