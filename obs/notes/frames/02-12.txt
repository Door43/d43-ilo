===== Naparuar Iti Hardin [02-12] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Ket imbaga ti Dios,”Itatta nagbalin a kas kadatayun dagiti tattao babaen iti pannakaammu da iti nasayaat ken kinadakes. Kasapulan a saan da a mapalubusan a mangan manen iti bunga iti kayo ti biag ken agbiag awan inggana.” Isua impaadayu ti Dios ni Adam ken Eve iti hardin. Ket pinabantay ti Dios iti dakkel ken mannakabalin nga anghel iti ruangan iti hardin tapnun awan iti uray maysa a makapangan ti bunga ti kayo iti biag.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  *

**like us by knowing good and evil**  - Here, this phrase points to a new way that Adam and Eve would be like God. Because they had sinned, they were aware of evil and could experience it. You could say, “because now they knew both good and evil.”

  *

**fruit**  - The specific kind of fruit is not revealed, so it is best to translate this using a general word for fruit.

  *

**tree of life**  - This was an actual tree with fruit. See **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/01-11|[01-11]]]**. If a person ate this fruit he would live continually and never die.
