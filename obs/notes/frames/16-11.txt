===== Ti Tagtagainep Ti Maysa A Midianita [16-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Iti dayta a rabii kinuna ti Dios kenni Gideon, “Mapan kayu iti kampo dagiti taga Midian ket nu mangngeg yo nu anya ti ibagbaga da. Saan kayun a mabuteng.” Isunga iti dayta a rabii ket bimmaba ni Gideon iti kampo ket nangngeg na ti suldado a taga Midian a mangibagbaga iti gayyem na maigapu iti natagtagainep na. Kinuna ti lalaki a gayem, “Ti kaipapanan daytoy a tagtagainep ket dagiti suldado ni Gideon ket parmiken da datayo a suldado ti taga Midian!” idi nangngeg ni Gideon daytoy ket nagdaydayaw ti Dios.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **Go down** - The Midianite soldiers were camping out in a valley and were at a lower elevation than the Israelite soldiers.
  * **you will no longer be afraid** – That is, "you will stop being afraid."
  * **something he had dreamed** – That is, "something he had seen in a dream" or, "a dream he had had."
