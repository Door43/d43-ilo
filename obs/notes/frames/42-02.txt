===== Nakipagna Ni Jesus Kadagiti Adalan [42-02] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Immasideg ni Jesus kadakuada ket rinuggian na ti nakipagpagna kadakuada ngem saan da a nailasin isuna. Dinamag na no anya ti pagtungtungtungan da ket ket imbaga da kenkuana dagiti amin a nakaskasdaaw a napasamak maipanggep kenni Jesus kadagiti napalpalabas nga aldaw. Impagarup da a makikatkatungtung da iti maysa a bisita a saan a makaammu iti napasamak idiay Jerusalem.

===== Dagiti Napapateg a Balikas: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus, Jesus Christ, Christ Jesus]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **did not recognize him**  - That is, "did not know that it was Jesus."
  * **all the remarkable things that had happened**  - This refers to the teaching and miracles of Jesus, his death, and the reports of his resurrection.
