===== Addan Iti Nakari A Daga [14-14] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan ti panagalla-alla dagiti Israelita idiay kabakbakiran iti las-ud ti uppat a pulo a tawen ket natay dagiti amin a nagsalungasing iti Dios. Kalpasan daytoy inturong manen ti Dios dagiti tattao iti igid ti Naikari a Daga. Lakay unayen ni Moses isua pinili ti Dios ni Joshua tapno tumulung kenkuana a mangidalan kadagiti tattao. Inkari ti Dios kenni Moses nga addanto ti aldaw a mangibaon isuna iti sabali a Mammadto a kas kenni Moses.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **who had rebelled against God** - This could be translated, "who had refused to obey God when he told them to go into the Promised Land."
  * **the people** - That is, the children of the generation that died.
  * **one day** - This means, "some time in the future."
  * **another prophet like Moses** - Like Moses, this man would be an Israelite, he would speak God's words to the people, and he would lead the people.
