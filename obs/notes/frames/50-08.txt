===== Ti Talun Ket Ti Lubong [50-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Saan a maawatan dagiti adalan na iti kayat na a saowen iti storya isua dinawat da ken Jesus nga ilawlawagna daytuy kadakuada. Kinuna ni Jesus. “Ti nagimula a tao kadagiti nasasayaat ket miarig a ti Messiah. Ti talun ket miarig iti lubong. Dagiti nasasayaat a bukel ket miarig kadagiti tattao iti pagarian ti Dios.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the people of God's kingdom** - This can also be translated as, "the people who live under God's authority" or, "the people who have submitted to God's rule" or, "the people who will live with God in his Kingdom."
