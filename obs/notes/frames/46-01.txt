===== Tiniliw Ni Saul Dagiti Namati [46-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Ni Saul ti lalaki a nangbantay kadigiti pagan-anay dagiti lallaki a nangpatay ken ni Stephen. Saan a mamati isuna kenni Jesus isu nga indadanes na dagiti namati. Sinagsaggaysa na dagiti babbalay idiay Jerusalem tapno tiliwen ken ipakulong na dagiti namati kenni Jesus. Inikkan isuna ti kangatoan a padi iti pammalubos a mapan idiay Damascus tapno tiliwenna dagiti amin a kristiano sadiay ken isubli na ida idiay Jerusalem.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen** - See **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:45-06|[45-06]]]**.
  * **from house to house** - That is, "into many houses."
  * **Damascus** - Damascus is now the capital of the country of Syria. During Saul's time, it was a city belonging to the Roman Empire. Most of the people there were not Jewish, but there were some Jews and also some Christians living there.
