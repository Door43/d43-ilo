===== Ayayaten Naka Ti Dios [49-13] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-13.jpg?nolink&}}

Isalakan ti Dios ti siasinoman a mamati ken mangawat kenni Jesus a kas Maestro da. Ngem saan na nga isalakan ti siasinoman a madi mamati kenkuana. Awan maitulung ti kinabaknang wennu kinapangraw, lalaki man wennu babae, kinataengan man wennu kinaubing, wennu sadinno man iti paggiagianam. Ayayaten naka ti Dios ken kayat na nga mamati ka kenni Jesus tapnu addaan isuna iti nabileg a pannakilangenlangen na kenka.

  * **he can have a close relationship with you** - This can also be translated as, "so that you can be his child" or, "so that you can be his friend" or, "so you can belong to him."
