===== Nabiag Ni Jesus [42-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Kinuna kadagiti dua a lallaki, “ni Jesus daydiay! Isu met laeng gayam a bumarbara dagiti puso ta idi ipalpalawag na ti sao ti Dios!” Nagsubli da a dagus idiay Jerusalem ket idi nakadanun dan, imbaga da kadagiti adalan, “Nakita mi ni Jesus! Sibibiag isuna!”

===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **our hearts were burning**  - That is, "we were getting excited" or, "we were beginning to hope" or, "we felt joy." Some languages have ways of expressing it such as, "our hearts were stirred" or, "our hearts were warmed." If you use an expression that refers to the heart burning, make sure it does not indicate that they were upset or angry.
