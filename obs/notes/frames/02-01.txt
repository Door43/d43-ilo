===== Awan Basol Iti Lubong [02-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Agbibiag ni Adam ken ti asawa na a siraragsak idiay hardin nga inaramid ti Dios para kadakuada. Ngem awan ti badbado da. Numan pay kasta ket saan da a nakarikna iti panagbain gapu ta awan pay laeng iti basol iti lubong. Kankanayun da a magmagna iti hardin ken makikatkatungtong iti Dios.

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **garden**  - A special collection of trees and plants that God prepared for Adam and Eve to enjoy and from which to eat. This should be the same word that was used in **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:01-11|[01-11]]]**. See how you translated it there.
  * **talked with God**  - The word for "talk" should be the same as would be used to refer to talking to a human being. God may have taken a physical form to talk to the man and the woman since the text indicates that they talked to him face-to-face.
  * **shame**  - The feeling that comes with knowing we have sinned or that we fall short in some way.
