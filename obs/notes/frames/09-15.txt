===== Tinulungan ni Aaron ni Moses [09-15] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-15.jpg?nolink&}}

Nagbuteng ni Moses ket saan nan a kayat a mapan kenni pharaoh ta saan isuna a nalaing a sumao. Ket gapu iti daytoy, imbaon ti Dios ni Aaron a kabsat ni Moses tapnon tulungan na ni Moses. Binallaagan ti Dios ni Moses ken Aaron a natangken ti ulo ni Pharaoh.

===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **was afraid and did not want to go**  - Moses knew that Pharaoh wanted to kill him, and he did not believe that he could do what God wanted him to do.
  * ** Nagbuteng... ket saan nan a kayat a mapan ** - Ammun ni Moses a kayat ni Pharaoh a patayin isuna ken saan na patien a kaya na nga aramiden ti ipaaramid ti Dios kanyana.
  * **Moses' brother, Aaron**  - Aaron was Moses's actual brother from his Israelite mother and father. Aaron would have been at least several years older than Moses.
  * ** ni Aaron a kabsat ni Moses ** - Agpayasu nga agkapsat da Aaron ken Moses babaen iti pada da nga ama ken nanang nga Israelita. Mas nataengan ni Aaron ngem ni Moses iti adu a tawen.
  * **stubborn**  - This means the Pharaoh would refuse to obey God. You may also want to add, "stubborn and refuse to listen (or obey)."
  * ** natangken ti ulo ** - Kayatna a saoen a saan nga agtulnog ni Pharaoh iti ibagan ti Dios. Mabalin nga inayon ti, "natangken ti ulo ni Pharaoh ken saan nga agdengngeg iti Dios."
  * //A Bible story from//  - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
