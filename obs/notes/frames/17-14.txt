===== Ti Pannakaiyanak Ni Solomon [17-14] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Natay ti ubing nga impasngay ni Batsheba kas dusa ni David iti nagbasulan. Adda pay ti panaglalaban ti kapamilyaan ni David iti amin a nabatbati a biag na. Kimmapsot iti kasta launay ti pannakabalin ni David nga agturay. Saanen a nagtulnog ni David iti Dios ket urayno kasta napudno latta ti Dios kadagiti inkari na. Iti saan a nagbayag naaddaan manen da David kenni Batsheba iti maysa nga anak a lalaki ket Solomon ti impanagan da.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **fighting in David's family** - This "fighting" was very serious. Among other things, one of his sons murdered another son and tried to take David's place as king while David was still reigning. If possible, use a word that conveys the intensity of this conflict within the family.
  * **David's power was greatly weakened** - This might be translated as, "David became less powerful" or, "David lost much of his authority."
