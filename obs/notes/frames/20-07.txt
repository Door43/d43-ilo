===== Dinadael dagiti taga Babylon iti taga Judah [20-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Ngem iti sumagmamanu a tawen ket nagrebelde a bimmusor ti ari iti Judah iti Babylon. Isu nga nagsubli dagiti taga Babylon ket dinarup da ti pagarian iti Judah. Tinagikua da ti siudad iti Jerusalem ken dinadael da ti templo ken inala da amin dagiti kinabaknang ti siudad ken ti templo.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **came back** - This may also be translated as, "returned" or "came to Judah again."
  * **captured the city** - That is, "took control of the city and the people there."
  * **took away** - They carried the treasures with them back to Babylon.
