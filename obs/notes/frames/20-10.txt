===== Nalagip ti Dios iti kari na [20-10] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Noman pay dinusa ti Dios dagiti tattao na Gapo iti basul da ken babaen iti panangipanaw na kadakuada ket saan na a nilipatan isuda wenno dagiti karkari na. Intultuloy a binanbantayan ti Dios dagiti tattao na ken nagtimek kadakuada babaen kadagiti mammadto na. Inkari na a kalpasan ti pitupulo a tawen ket agsubli danto manen iti Naikari a Daga.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **exile** - See how you translated this term in **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]**.
  * **did not forget** - This could be translated as, "did not ignore" or, "did not neglect." Or the whole phrase could be translated as, "continued to honor his commitment to his people and his promises."
  * **watch over** - That is, "take care of."
  * **speak to them through his prophets** - This could be translated as, "tell his prophets messages that he wanted them to tell his people."
