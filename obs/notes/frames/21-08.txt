===== Agturay Ti Mesias Iti Agnanayon [21-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Ti ari ket maysa a mangituray iti pagarian ken mangukom kadagiti tattao. Ti Messiah ti naan-anay nga ari nga agtugawto iti tono ti kapuonan na a ni David. Iturayannanto ti entero a sangalubongan iti agnanayon ken kankanayonto isuna a mangukom a sipupudno ken agaramid kadagiti umno a pangngeddeng.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **sit on the throne of his ancestor David** - A throne is a ceremonial chair for a king or queen that represents their authority. This expression could also be translated as, "have the authority to reign as his ancestor King David did" or, "be the descendant of King David and continue his rule over God's people."
  * **the whole world** - This could also be translated as, "everyone everywhere."
