===== Jacob Met Esau [07-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Nakaro unay ti buteng ni Jacob gapu ta impagarupna a kayat pay ni Esau a patayen isuna. Isua nangipatulod ti nakaad'adu nga ay'ayup a kas sagot na ken Esau. Dagiti adipen a nangipan kadagiti ay'ayup ket kinuna da kenni Esau, "Ni Jacob nga adipen mo ti nangted kadagitoy nga ayayup. Umay to isuna iti mabiit."

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **your servant, Jacob** - Jacob was not actually Esau's servant. But Jacob told his servants to say this because he wanted to show Esau that he was coming to him humbly and respectfully, so that Esau would no longer be angry with him.
