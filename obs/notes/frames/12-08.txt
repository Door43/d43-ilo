===== Bimmallasiw Dagiti Israelita Iti Baybay [12-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Nagna dagiti Israelita iti namaga a daga idiay tengnga ti nalabbaga a baybay nga addaan iti pader a danum iti agsinnumbangir na.

===== Dagiti Napapateg A Balikas: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:israel|Israel, Israelites, nation of Israel]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita : =====

  * **marched**  - This can be translated as, "walked" or, "went."
  * **with a wall of water on either side of them**  - This could be translated as, "and the water on both sides of them stood up tall and straight like a wall."
