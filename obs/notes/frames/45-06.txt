===== Dinarup Dagiti Tattao Dagiti Namati [45-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Adda maysa a lalaki nga agnagan iti Saul ti immanamong kadagiti tattao a nangpapatay kenni Stephen. Binantayan na dagiti pagan-anayda kabayatan nga ub-uburen da ni Stephen. Iti dayta nga aldaw, adu dagiti tattao idiay Jerusalem ti nangrugi a nangidadanes kadagiti pasurot ni Jesus isua pimmanaw dagiti namati ket napanda kadagiti sabali a luglugar. Ngem laksid iti daytoy intultuluy dagiti namati nga inkaskasaba ti maipapan kenni Jesus iti amin a napnapananda.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **guarded their robes**  - This could be translated as, "watched over their coats." He was probably making sure they didn't get stolen or damaged.
  * **But in spite of this**  - The Jewish leaders thought they could stop the spread of Jesus' teaching by persecuting his followers. Instead, this caused them to scatter and spread the message even more widely.
