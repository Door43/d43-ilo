===== Dinarup Dagiti Tattau Dagiti Namati [45-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Adda maysa a barito nga agnagan iti Saul a immanugot kadagiti tattao nga nangpapatay kenni Stephen. Isuna ti nagbantay kadagiti pagananayda kabayatan a barbarsakenda iti bato ni Stephen. Iti dayta nga aldaw ket uyuyawen ti adu a tattao idiay Jerusalem dagiti pasurot ni Jesus. Isua pimmanaw dagiti namati ket napanda iti dadduma a luglugar. Ngem ti likudan amin dagitoy ket intultuluy dagiti namati a inkaskasaba  iti maipapan kenni Jesus iti amin a papananda.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **guarded their robes** - This could be translated as, "watched over their coats." He was probably making sure they didn't get stolen or damaged.
  * **But in spite of this** - The Jewish leaders thought they could stop the spread of Jesus' teaching by persecuting his followers. Instead, this caused them to scatter and spread the message even more widely.
