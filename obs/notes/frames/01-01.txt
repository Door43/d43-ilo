===== The Beginning [01-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-01.jpg?nolink&}}

This is how **the beginning** of everything happened. God **created** the **universe** and everything in it in six days. After God created the earth it was **dark** and **empty**, and **nothing had been formed** in it. But **God's Spirit** was there over the water.
===== Important Terms:Dagiti Importante a Sasao: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:god|God/ Dios]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:holyspirit|God's Spirit/ Ispiritun ti Dios ]]**
==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **the beginning**  - That is, "the beginning of all things," before anything existed except God./ **ti rugrugina**  - Ti kayat na a saowen ket "idi rugrugin iti amin a banbanag," idi awan pay ti amin except ti Dios.
  * **created**  - This is used here in the sense of making something out of nothing./ **pinarsua**  - iti kayat na a ipakaamo ditoy ket panag'aramid ti bagay nga awan paunnan.
  * **universe**  - This includes everything that God created on the earth and in the heavens, both visible and invisible things./ **Lubong**  - Sakupen na daytoy amin a pinarsuan ti Dios ditoy daga ken sadiay langit.
  * **earth**  - The word "earth" refers to the whole world that people live on./ **daga**  - daytoy sao a "daga" ket agraman ti lubong a pagyanan iti tattao.
  * **dark**  - It was completely dark. There was no light at all, since God had not yet created light./ **sipnget**  - pulos nga nasipnget. Awan pulos ti nalawag ta saan pay nga pinarsuan ti Dios iti lawag.
  * **empty**  - God had not yet created anything except for the bare earth covered by water./ **awan linaom**  - Awan pay pinarsuan ti Dios malaksid iti __awan pulos nagyan__  daga nga nabalutan ti danum.
  * **nothing had been formed**  - There were no distinguishing featuresâ€“just water covering everything./ **awan pay naporma**  - Awan ti mailasig/makita a porma malaksid ti danom a nagbalot ti daga.
  * **God's Spirit**  - The Spirit of God, sometimes called the Holy Spirit, was present in the beginning, moving freely over the earth in order to create all that he had planned to do./ **Ispiriton ti Dios**  - Ti Ispiriton ti Dios ket sagpaminsan ket makuna a Santo Ispirito, ket adda idi panunganay nga siwayawaya a umalis'alis iti daga tapno parsuaenna amin plano na nga aramiden.
