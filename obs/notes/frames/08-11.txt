===== Nangisangpet Ti Makan Dagiti Agkakapsat [08-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Isua imbaun ni Jacob dagiti inauna nga annak na idiay Egypt tapnu gumatang ti makan. Ket saan a nailasin dagiti agkakabsat ni Joseph idi gumatgatang da iti makan iti sanguanan da. Ngem nalasin ni Joseph ida.

===== Dagiti Napapateg A Balikas: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:jacob|Jacob, Israel]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:egypt|Egypt, taga Egypt]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:josephot|Joseph (OT)]]**

==== **Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita **: ====

  * **his older sons**  - These were Joseph's older brothers who had sold him into slavery.
  * **did not recognize Joseph**  - They did not know that the man was Joseph, because now Joseph was much older than when they last saw him, and he was dressed as an Egyptian ruler.
