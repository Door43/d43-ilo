===== Inbaun Ti Dios Ni Ananias Kenni Saul [46-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Adda idi ti maysa nga adalan idiay Damascus nga agnagan iti Ananias. Imbagan ti Dios kenkuana, “Mapan ka iti balay a pagnanaedan ni Saul. Ipatay mo dagita imam kenkuana tapno makakita manen isuna.” Ngem kinuna ni Ananias, “Apo, nadamag ko nu kasano ti panangidaddadanes daytoy a lalaki kadagiti namati.” Ngem simmungbat ti Dios, “Apan ka! Ta pinilik isuna a mangiwaragawag iti nagan ko kadagiti Judio ken kadagiti tattao manipud kadagiti dadduma a tribu. Adunto ti sagabaenna a banag para iti nagan ko.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But Ananias said**  - If it is not clear why the word "But" is used here, this could be translated as, "But Ananias was afraid, so he said."
  * **God answered him**  - This could be translated as, "To reassure Ananias, God said" to make clear why God said this.
  * **to declare my name**  - That is, "to teach about me" or, "to make me known."
  * **for my name**  - That is, "for me" or, "because of me" or, "because he serves me" or, "because he teaches others about me."
