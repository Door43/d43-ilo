===== God Created the Land [01-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg?nolink&}}

On the **third day**, **God spoke** and separated the water from the dry land. He called the dry land “**earth**,” and he called the water “seas.” God saw that what he had **created** was **good**.

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:god|God/ Dios]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:good|good/ Nasaat]]**
==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **third day**  - The next in the series of orderly days in which God prepared the earth for life. **maikatlo nga aldaw**  - Ti sumaruno iti nasarsaruno naurnos a sagmamano nga aldaw a nagsaganaan ti Dios iti daga para ti bia.
  * **God spoke**  - God created the dry land by speaking a command. **nagsao ti Dios******- Pinarsuan ti Dios ti namaga a daga babaen iti pagsasao a mandar.
  * **earth**  - This word is used here to refer to dirt or soil, which is what dry land is made of. **daga**  - Daytoy a sao ket sakopen na ti pitak.
  * **created**  - This word is used here in the sense of making something out of nothing. **pinarsua**  - Daytoy a sao ket nausar iti panag'aramid ti banbanag nga awan mausar.
