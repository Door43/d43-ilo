===== Nagungar Ni Jesus [41-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Idin nakadanunen dagiti babbai iti tanem ket kinuna ti anghel kadakuada, “Saan kayo nga agbuteng. Awanen ditoy ni Jesus ta nagungaren manipud kadagiti natay kas iti imbaga na a mapasamak. Kitaen yo ti tanem tapno mapaneknekan yo.” Kinita dagiti babbai ti tanem a nakaikabilan ti bagi ni Jesus ngem awanen ti bagi na sadiay!

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not be afraid**  - That is, "Stop being afraid." An angel shining like lightning was a frightening sight!
  * **He has risen from the dead**  - This could be translated as, "He has come back to life."
