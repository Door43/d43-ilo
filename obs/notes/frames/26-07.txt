===== Pinanggepda A Papatayen Ni Jesus [26-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Inruar dagiti tattao a taga Nazareth ni Jesus manipud iti lugar a pagdaydayawan ket impanda isuna iti igid ti teppang tapno patayen da babaen iti panangitappuak da kenkuana sadiay. Ngem nagna ni Jesus iti tengnga dagiti adu a tattao ket pinanawan na ti ili ti Nazareth.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **place of worship** - That is, "the building where the Jewish people gathered to worship God." This can also be translated as "worship building." See how you translated it in **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:26-02|[26-02]]]**.
  * **But Jesus walked through the crowd** - "But" could be translated with a strong contrast word or phrase such as "But instead" or, "However" to show that the people were not able to do to Jesus what they had planned to do.
