===== Inkasaba Ni Saul Ti Maipapan Kenni Jesus [46-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Iti dayta met laeng a kanito nangrugi ni Saul a nangaskasaba kadagiti Judio idiay Damascus. Kinunana, “Ni Jesus ti Anak ti Dios!”  Nasdaaw dagiti Judio gapo ta ti tao a nangpadas a mangdadael kadagiti namati ket maysa metten a namati ken ni Jesus! Nangasaba ni Saul kadagti Judio ken pinaneknekan na a ni Jesus ti Messiah.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Right away**  - This can also be translated as, "Immediately" or, "Then."
  * **reasoned with the Jews**  - This could also be translated as, "gave the Jews good reasons for believing in Jesus" or, "spoke persuasively with the Jews."
