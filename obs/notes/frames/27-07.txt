===== Ti Levita [27-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Saan a mabayag kalpasan dayta ket simmalog met ti maysa a levita. (Dagiti levita ket maysa a tribu dagiti Judio a tumultulong kadagiti papadi idiay templo.) Bimmallasiw met laeng ti Levita iti bangir ti dalan ken linabsan na diay lalaki a natakawan ken nadangran.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell the story.)
  * **came down the road** - That is, "was traveling down that same road."
  * **Levites were a tribe of Jews** - That is, "Levites were from the Israelite tribe of Levi" or, "Levites were from the Israelite clan of Levi."
  * **ignored** - That is, "did not help."
