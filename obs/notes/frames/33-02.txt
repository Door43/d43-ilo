===== Ti Bukel Iti Dalan [33-02] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Imbaga ni Jesu daytoy nga istorya, “Rimmuar ti maysa a mannalon tapno agmula iti bukel. Kas ipurpurwak na dagiti bukel ket nagdisso dagiti dadduma iti dalan ket immay dagiti billit ket kinnan da amin dagidiay a bukel.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus, Jesus Christ, Christ Jesus]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita : =====

  * **spreading the seeds by hand**  - That is, "throwing the seeds on the soil" or, "covering all of his crop soil with seeds." This is the way that farmers in the ancient Middle East typically planted grain-bearing crops.
