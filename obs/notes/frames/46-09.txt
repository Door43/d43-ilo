===== Dagiti Christiano Idiay Antioch [46-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Adda dagiti namati a naidadanes idiay Jerusalem nga naglibas a napan idiay Antioch ket nagtululuy da a nangaskasaba maipapan kenni Jesus. Kaadwan kadagiti tattao idiay Antioch ket saan nga Judio, ngem iti umuna nga gundaway, adu kadakuada iti nagbalin met laeng nga namati. Napan ni Barnabas ken Saul idiay tapnun surwan da dagitoy a baru nga namati iti adu nga maipanggep kenni Jesus ken tapnun mapabileg da iti Iglesia. Ti immuna nga maawagan nga “Kristiano” ket dagiti namati ken Jesus idiay Antioch.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **city of Antioch** - This was an ancient city located in what is now the southernmost tip of the modern country of Turkey, near its border with Syria and close to the Mediterranean Sea. It was about 450 miles northwest of Jerusalem.
  * **strengthen the church** - This could also be translated as, "help the church to grow strong spiritually" or, "help the believers in Jesus to grow strong in their faith" or, "help the people to believe more firmly in Jesus."
