===== Ti Pagtaengan Ti Nangato A Padi [39-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Katengngaanen ti rabii idi inyapan dagiti suldado ni Jesus idiay balay ti kangatoan a padi tapno mausig isuna. Sumursurot ni Peter kadakuada manipud iti adayo. Idin inserrekdan ni Jesus iti balay ket nagtalinaed ni Peter iti ruar ken napan naginudo iti asideg ti apuy.

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus, Jesus Christ, Christ Jesus]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:highpriest|high priest]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:peter|Peter, Simon Peter, Cephas]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the middle of the night**  - This expression means, "halfway through the night" or, "very late at night."
  * **to question him**  - That is, "to ask Jesus questions to try to find something they could accuse Jesus of doing wrong."
