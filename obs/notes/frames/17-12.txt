===== Pinapatay Ni David Ni Uriah [17-12] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Iti asawa ni Bathsheba ket agnagan ti Uriah a maysa kadagiti suldado ni David a kalalaingan. Impaayab ni David ni Uriah  manipud idiay paggubatan ket imbaga na nga agawid tapnu kadwaan na ti asawa na. Ngem madi kayat ni Uriah nga agawid kabayatan a dagiti kakadwa na a suldado ket makigubgubat. Isua impatulod ni David ni Uriah iti paggugubatan ket imbaga na iti heneral nga ikabil da isuna  nu sadinno ti kapigpigsaan kadagiti kabusor tapnun mapapatay isuna.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **go be with his wife** - That is, "go home to be intimate with his wife." David wanted people, especially Uriah, to believe that Bathsheba was pregnant with Uriah's child.
  * **where the enemy was strongest** - That is, the location in the battle where the most fighting was going on.
