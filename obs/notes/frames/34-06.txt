===== Ti Dua A Lalaki Iti Templo [34-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan daytoy ket sinarita ni Jesus ti maysa nga istorya kadagiti dadduma a tattao nga agtaltalek iti bukod da a nasayaat nga ar-aramid ken mangdildillaw kadagiti dadduma a tattao. Kinuna na ket “Adda dua a lallaki a napan nagkararag idiay templo. Ti maysa kadakuada ket agsingsingir iti buwis ket ken diay maysa ket panguloen iti relihiyon.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **story** - See how you translated this term in **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:34-01|[34-01]]]**.
  * **trusted in their own good deeds** - That is, "believed that their good deeds made them righteous" or, "were proud of how perfectly they kept God's law" or, "believed that their good deeds caused God to be completely pleased with them."
  * **despised other people** - That is, "considered other people to be inferior to them" or, "looked down on other people."
