===== Pinarmek Ti Dios Dagiti Midianita [16-14] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Tinikaw ti Dios dagiti taga Midian isua nagraranget ken nagpipinnatay ti tunggal maysa kadakuada. Kadayta met laeng a kanito ket inayaban dagiti dadduma nga Israelita dagiti nabati kadagiti balbalay da tapno kamaten da dagiti taga Midian. Nagadu dagiti pinatay da ken kinamat ken pinapanaw da dagiti nabati pay iti daga dagiti Israelita. Natay ti sanga gasut ken dua a pulo a ribu kadagiti taga Midian iti dayta nga aldaw.  Insalakan ti Dios dagiti Israelitas.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **God confused the Midianites** - God caused the Midianites to be confused. They wanted to attack the Israelites, but instead, they attacked each other.
  * **the rest of the Israelites** - This could be translated as, "many other Israelite men." This refers to the soldiers previously sent home in **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:16-10|[16-10]]]**.
