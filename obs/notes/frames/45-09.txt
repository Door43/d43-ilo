===== Nasabat Ni Philip Dagiti Ethiopian [45-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Sinaludsod ni Philip iti Ethiopian, “Maawawatam kadi ta basbasaem?” Sungbat ti taga Ethiopia ket kunana, “Saan. Madik maawatan daytoy aginggana adda ti mangilawlawag  kanyak. Pangngaasim ta umay ka a makitugaw kanyak. Insurat kadi ni Isaiah ti panggep iti bagina wennu iti dadduma?”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Was Isaiah writing about** - This can also be translated as, "Was Isaiah referring to."
