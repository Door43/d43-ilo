===== Napan ni Jacob idiay Egypt [08-14] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Urayno lakayen ni Jacob ket immalis latta isuna idiay Egypt  karamanen dagiti amin a pamilya na ket nakipagnaed da amin idiay. Sakbay a natay ni Jacob ket binendisyonan na ti tunggal maysa kadagiti annak na.

**Dagiti Napapateg A Balikas**:

  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:egypt|Egypt, taga Egypt]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:bless|bendisyonan, nabendisyonan, bendisyon]]**

==== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: ====

  * **Even though Jacob was an old man, he moved to Egypt**  - Egypt was a great distance away from Canaan, and it would have been difficult for an elderly man to walk or ride a cart that far.
  * **Before Jacob died**  - Jacob died in Egypt. He did not get to return to Canaan, the land God had promised to give to him and his descendants.
