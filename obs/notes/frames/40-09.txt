===== Ti Pannakaitanem Ni Jesus [40-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan daytoy, dua a panguloen dagiti Judio a namati kenni Jesus nga agnagan iti Joseph ken Nicodemus ti nangkiddaw kenni Pilato nga alaen da ti bagi ni Jesus. Binalkot da ti bagi na iti lupot ken inkabil da isuna iti tanem a bato. Kalpasanna, intulid da ti dakkel a bato iti sango ti tanem tapno maserraan daytoy.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Joseph**  - This was not Mary's husband. It was another man named Joseph.
  * **asked Pilate for Jesus' body**  - That is, "asked Pilate to permit them to take Jesus' body down from the cross."
