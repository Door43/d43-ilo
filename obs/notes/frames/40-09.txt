===== Ti Pannakaitanem Ni Jesus [40-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Santo dinawat ni Joseph ken Nicodenus kenni Pilato ti bagi ni Jesus. Dagitoy a panguluen a Judio ket mamati a ni Jesus ket isu ti Mesias. Ket binalkut da iti bagi na iti luput santo da inyapan iti tanem nakali iti batu. Sato da intulid ti dakkel a batu iti ruwangan ti tanem tapnu masarraan.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Joseph** - This was not Mary's husband. It was another man named Joseph.
  * **asked Pilate for Jesus' body** - That is, "asked Pilate to permit them to take Jesus' body down from the cross."
