===== Dagiti Dua A Spiya [15-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Tiempo na metten a sumrek dagiti Israelita idiay Canaan a Naikari a Daga. Nangibaon ni Joshua iti dua nga espiya idiay Jerico a siudad ti Canaan a nalikmutan iti natibker a pader. Iti dayta a siudad ket adda agnanaed a maysa a balangkantis nga agnagan iti Rahab. Inlemmeng ken tinulongan na dagiti espiya tapno makalibas da. Inaramid na daytoy gapu ta mamati isuna iti Dios. Inkari dagiti espiya a salakniban da ni Rahab ken ti pamilya na inton dadaelen dagiti Israelita ti Jerico.

==== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita : ====

  * **At last it was time for**  - "At last" means "finally" or, "after a long wait." To make it clear what "time" refers to, you could say, "after they had wandered in the desert for 40 years, God finally permitted."
  * **two spies to the Canaanite city of Jericho**  - This could be translated as, "two men to Jericho, a city in Canaan, to find out information about it." Also see the notes for, "spy out the land" in **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:14-04|[14-04]]]**.
  * **protected by strong walls**  - This could be translated as, "completely surrounded by thick, strong walls made of stone to protect it from their enemies."
