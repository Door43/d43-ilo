===== Nagbasul Ni David [17-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Imbes a kumita iti sabali ket nangibaun ni David iti tao tapnun iyapan da iti babai kenkuana. Nakikaidda ni David kenkuana santu impatulod na isuna nga agawid diay balay da. Iti nabiit a tiempo ket nangipatulod ni Bathsheba iti damag kenni David a nangipadamag a masikug  isuna.

===== Important Terms: =====


  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:david]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:bathsheba]]**


==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **Instead of looking away** - That is, David did not look away from the woman as she bathed, as he should have done.
  * **He slept with her** - This is a polite way to say that David had sexual relations with Bathsheba.
