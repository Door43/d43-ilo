===== Ibagam Kadagiti Adalan [41-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan na, kinuna ti anghel kadagiti babbae, “Mapan yo ibaga kadagiti adalan a ‘nagungaren ni Jesus manipud iti patay ken umun-unan isuna ngem kadakayo a mapan idiay Galilea.”

===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **he will go to Galilee ahead of you **- That is, "he will meet you in Galilee" or, "he will be in Galilee when you get there." "You" here is plural, including the apostles and other disciples.
