===== Ti Pannakaagas Ni Naaman [19-15] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-15.jpg?nolink&}}

Idi unona na ket nakaunget ni Naaman ken madi na kayat nga aramiden daytoy ta kasla maysa a minamaag. Ngem binaliwan na met laeng ti panonot na ket napan na intapug iti namin pitu a daras ti bagi na idiay karayan ti Jordan. Iti maudi a daras ket naagasan a naan-anay ti kudil na. Inagasan ti Dios isuna.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **would not do it because it seemed foolish** - Naaman would not do what Elisha said because he knew that washing alone could not heal his disease.
  * **he changed his mind** - That is, "he decided to do what Elisha had told him to do."
