===== Ti Pannakaagas Ni Naaman [19-15] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-15.jpg?nolink&}}

Idi ununa na ket agununget ni Naaman ket madi na kayat nga aramiden ti kasla a kinamaag. Ngem madamdama ket binaliwan na ti panunot na ket intabsaw na iti mamin pitu ti bagi na idiay karayan a Jordan. Idi rimmuar iti maudi ket naananay ti pannakaagas ti kudil na. Inagasan ti Dios isuna.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **would not do it because it seemed foolish** - Naaman would not do what Elisha said because he knew that washing alone could not heal his disease.
  * **he changed his mind** - That is, "he decided to do what Elisha had told him to do."
