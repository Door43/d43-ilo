===== Ti Lima A Tinapay Ken Iti Duwa A Lames [30-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Ngem imbaga ni Jesus kadagiti adalan na, “Ikkan yu ida ti mabalin da a makan!” Ket ti insungbat da ket kuna da, “Kasanu mi nga aramiden dayta?  Ti adda laeng kada kami ket lima a pisi ti tinapay ken dua a babassit a lames.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:disciple]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **How can we do that?** - This could be translated as, "We cannot do that!" or, "It is impossible to do that!" The disciples were not asking an actual question. Rather, they were strongly expressing that they did not think this was possible.
