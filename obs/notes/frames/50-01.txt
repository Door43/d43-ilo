===== Dumakdakkel Ti Iglesia [50-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Agarup dua a ribon a tawen ket umado nga umado dagiti tattao iti entero a lubong ti makangkangngeg iti naimbag a damag maipapan kenni Jesus a Messiah. Dumakdakkel mettenen ti Iglesia. Inkari ni Jesus nga agsublinto isuna inton gibus ti lubong. Uray nu saan pay a nagsubli isuna, tungpalennanto ti inkarina.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Church has been growing**  - This could also be translated as, "The number of people in the Church around the world has been increasing" or, "The number of believers in Jesus has been increasing."
  * **at the end of the world**  - This phrase means, "right before the present world comes to an end" or, "in the final days of this world."
