===== Mammadtu, Padi Ken Ari [21-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Dagiti mammadtu ti Dios ket imbaga da a ti Messiah ket agbalin a mammadtu ken padi ken ari. Ti mammadtu  ket tao a makangkangngeg iti sao ti Dios ket iwarwaragawag na ti sasao ti Dios kadagiti tattao. Ti Messiah nga inkari ti Dios nga ibaun na ket isu ti naananay  a mammadtu.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **perfect prophet** - The Messiah would be a prophet who is perfect in obedience to God, giving every word God would speak to the people. He would present God perfectly to the people, helping them to know and understand God.
