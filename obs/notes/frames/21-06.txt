===== Dagiti Mammadto, Padi Ken Ari [21-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Imbaga met dagiti mammadto ti Dios a ti Messiah ket maysanto a mammadto ket padi ken ari. Ti mammadto ket maysa a tao a makangkangngeg iti sao ti Dios ken iwarwaragawag na dagiti sasao ti Dios kadagiti tattao. Ti Messiah nga inkari ti Dios nga ibaun na ket naan-anay a mammadto.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **perfect prophet** - The Messiah would be a prophet who is perfect in obedience to God, giving every word God would speak to the people. He would present God perfectly to the people, helping them to know and understand God.
