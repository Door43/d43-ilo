===== Nagsao Ti God [14-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Ket kasta launay ti unget ti Dios ket napan idiay pagguummungan a tolda ket kinuna, “Gapu kumontra kayu kanyak ket aminen a tattao ket agalla’alla idiay kabakbakiran. Malaksid ni Joshua ken Caleb. Siasinnoman iti agtawen iti dua a pulo wennu natataengan pay ket matay ket saan a pulos a makastrek iti Naikari a Daga.”

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **came to** - God did not appear as a person, but came in some other form that showed his glory and power.
  * **wander in the wilderness** - God would lead the people around in the wilderness with no specific destination until all of the adults who rebelled against him died.
