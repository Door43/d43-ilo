===== Ti Sipnget [10-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasanna nangibaon ti Dios iti sipnget a nagpaut iti tallo nga aldaw. Nagsipnget launay isua saan a makaruar dagiti taga Egypt kadagiti babbalay. Ngem adda lawag iti pagnanaedan dagiti Israelita.

===== Dagiti Napapateg A Balikas: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:god|God]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:egypt|Egypt, Egyptian ]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:israel|Israel, Israelites, nation of Israel ]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **God sent darkness**  - God caused darkness to cover or spread over most of Egypt. In other words, God took away the light from this part of Egypt.
  * **darkness that lasted for three days**  - This darkness was darker than ordinary nighttime darkness, and it continued to be totally dark for three whole days.
