===== Pinatay Ni David Ni Goliath [17-03] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Nagbalin ni David a nasiglat a suldado ken mangidadaulo. Idi bumaro pay lang ni David ket linabanan na iti higante nga agnagan ti Goliath. Ni Goliath ket maysa a nasursuruan a suldado ken nakapigpigsa ken dandani a tallu metro ti katayag na. Ngem tinulungan ti Dios ni David ket pinatay na ni Goliath ken insalakan na ti Israel. Kalpasan dayta ket adu pay dagiti inababak na a dadduma a kabusor ti Israel nga isu ti nangidayawan ti tattao kanyana.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **a giant named Goliath** - The word "giant" here describes a person who is unusually large and powerful. Goliath was a huge soldier in an army that was fighting against Israel.
