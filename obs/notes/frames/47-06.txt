===== Nagunget Dagiti Tattao [47-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Nakaunget a permi ti lalaki nga amo ti tagabo a babae! Naamiris da a nu awan ti demonyo awan metten ti kabaelan ti babae a mangipadto iti masakbayan dagiti tattao. Saan metten nga agbayad dagiti tattao iti amo ti babae para iti pannakaipadto ti mabalin a mapasamak kadakuada.

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:servant|servant, slave, slavery]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **without the demon**  - That is, "without the power from the demon" or, "now that she did not have the demon."
  * **the future**  - That is, "what would happen to them in the future."
  * **This meant that**  - This could also be translated as, "Because of this" or, "Therefore."
