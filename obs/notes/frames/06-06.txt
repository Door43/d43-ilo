===== Duwa a Nasyon [06-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Imbaga ti Dios kenni Rebekah, "Duwwa a nasyon ti maadda manipud dita duwwa nga anak mo a lallaki nga adda dita buksit mo. Agpinnarigat da a duwwa ket pagserbiyan ton diay mas natataengan diay ububing."

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **from the two sons** - That is, "from the descendants of the two sons."
  * **They will struggle with each other** - Both the two sons and the nations that will come from them will continually fight with each other. Compare to **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:06-05|[06-05]]]**.
  * **the older son** - Even though the babies were twins, the baby that came out first would be treated as the older son.
