===== Ti Bunggoy Dagiti Babbabuy [32-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Nagpakaasi ti demonyo kenni Jesus, “Pangaasim ta saan na kami nga ibaon iti ruar daytoy a lugar! Adda idi ti adu a baboy a mangmangan iti asideg a turod isu a nagpakaasi dagiti demonyo kenni Jesus ket kunada, “Pangaasim ta ibaon na kami laengen kadagiti baboy!” Pinalubosan ida ni Jesus ket kunana, “Sige ket Mapan kayo!”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:demon|demon, evil spirit, unclean spirit]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:beg|beg, beggar ]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus, Jesus Christ, Christ Jesus]]**
===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita : =====

  * **do not send us**  - That is, "do not make us go."
  * **Please send us**  - That is, "Please let us go."
  * **instead**  - That is, "instead of making us go away."
  * **Go!**  - This could be translated as, "Go into the pigs!" or, "You may go into the pigs!"
