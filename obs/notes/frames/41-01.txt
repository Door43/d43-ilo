===== Ti Suldado Iti Tanem [41-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan nga inlansa dagiti suldado ni Jesus, imbaga dagiti saan a namati a panguloen dagiti Judio kenni Pilato, “imbaga idi dayta ulbod a Jesus nga agungarto isuna manipud iti patay kalpasan ti tallo nga aldaw. Kasapulan nga adda ti mangbantay iti tanem na tapno masigurado a saan a takawen dagiti adalan na ti bagi na ken ibaga da a nagungaren manipud iti ipapatay na.”

===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **the unbelieving Jewish leaders**  - That is, "the Jewish leaders who did not believe in Jesus."
  * **That liar, Jesus, said**  - This could also be translated as, "That man, Jesus, lied and said." They refused to believe that Jesus told the truth about being the Son of God.
  * **rise from the dead**  - That is, "come back to life" or, "become alive again."
