===== Saan A Nagbasol Isuna [39-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan a nakisarita kenni Jesus ket rimmuar ni Pilato ket napan kadagiti adu a tattao ket kinunana, “Awan ti makitak a nagbasulan daytoy a tao.” Ngem impukkaw dagiti panguloen a Judio ken dagiti tattao, “Ilansam isuna iti krus!” Simmungbat ni Pilato ket kinunana, “Saan a nagbasol daytoy tao,” Ngem pinigpigsaan da pay ti panagpukkaw da. Ket imbaga ni Pilato iti maikatlo a daras, “Saan a nagbasol daytoy tao!”

===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **I find no guilt in this man**  - That is, "I do not find this man to be guilty" or, "I have examined this man, and I do not see that he has done anything wrong."
  * **He is not guilty**  - That is, "He has done nothing wrong!"
