===== Pinerdi Ni Gideon Ti Didiosen [16-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Addaan ti ama ni Gideon iti pagidatonan a para iti maysa a didiosen. Imbaga ti Dios kenni Gideon a perdien na dayta a pagidatonan. Ngem mabuteng ni Gideon kadagiti tattao isua naguray isuna agingga a rimmabii sananto binurak ti pagidatonan ken rinumek-rumek. Nangipatakder isuna iti baro a pagidatonan a para iti Dios iti abay ti dati a pagidatonan a para iti didiosen. Kalpasan daytoy, nagidaton isuna ditoy iti para iti Dios.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **to tear down** - This means, "to violently bring down" or, "to take down and destroy."
  * **afraid of the people** - Gideon was afraid that his fellow Israelites who worshiped the same idol would be angry with him.
  * **waited until nighttime** - Another way to say this would be, "waited until after dark." Gideon smashed the altar at night when everyone was asleep so that no one would see him do it.
