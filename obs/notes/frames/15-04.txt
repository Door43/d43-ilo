===== Panagpukkaw Iti Dios [15-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Ket iti maikapito nga aldaw nagmartsa manen dagiti Israelita iti aglawlaw ti siudad iti namin pito a daras. Bayat ti panagmartsa da iti aglawlaw ti siudad idi madanonen ti maudi a daras ket nagpukkaw dagiti suldado kabayatan a pinuyutan met dagiti papadi dagiti trumpeta da.

===== Important Terms: =====


  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:israel]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:priest]]**


==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **While they marched around the city for the last time** - This can be translated as, "During their final circuit around the city."
  * **blew their trumpets** - This could be translated as, "They sounded their trumpets" or, "They played their trumpets." These trumpets were made out of ram's horns.
