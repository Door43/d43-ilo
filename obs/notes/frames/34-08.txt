===== Nasayaatak [34-08] =====

“Kas kuma iti panagayunar ko iti mamin duwa iti maysa a lawas, ken panangted ko iti apagka pullo iti amin a kuwarta ken dagiti amin a naimmateryalan a maaw-awat ko.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:fast]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues to tell of the religious leader's prayer.)
  * **I fast** - The religious ruler believed that doing this would earn favor with God.
  * **ten percent** - That is, "one-tenth."
