===== Ti Panaganak Ti Birhen [21-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Adu pay dagiti nga impadto dagiti mammadto ti Dios maipapan iti Messiah. Impadto ni mammadto a Malachi addanto umay a nabileg a mammadto sakbay ti Messiah. Impadto met ni mammadto nga Isaiah a maiyanakto ti Messiah babaen iti maysa a birhen. Imbaga met ni mammadto a Micah a maiyanakto ti Messiah iti ili ti Bethlehem.

  * **Malachi** - Malachi was the last prophet in the Old Testament.
  * **foretold** - This could be translated as, "predicted" or, "prophesied." It means to tell something that will happen in the future. Malachi spoke God's message to the people 400 years before the Messiah came.
