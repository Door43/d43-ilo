===== Limmaban Ti Dios Para Kadagiti Israelita [12-10] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Isua sinurot da dagiti Israelita iti dalan idiay baybay, ngem inaramid ti Dios dagiti taga Egypt a mataranta da ken pinailumlom na dagiti karwahe da. Nagpupukkaw dagiti taga Egypt a kunu da, “Agtaray tayu! Ta ti Dios ket isu iti makilablaban kadagiti Israelita!”

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **path through the sea** - This was the dry strip of land across the bottom of the sea, with a wall of water on each side.
  * **to panic** – This could be translated as, "to get fearful and confused."
  * **get stuck** - The chariots could no longer move.
