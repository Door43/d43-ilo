Pinarsuan Ti Dios Ti Tangatang [01-03]

obs-en-01-03.jpg

Ti maikadwa nga aldaw ket nagsao ti Dios ket naparsua ti tangatang iti rabaw ti daga. Inaramid na ti tangatang babaen iti panagsina na iti danum sadiay akin ngatu ken dadiay danum nga adda sadiay baba.

On the second day of creation, God spoke and created the sky above the earth. He made the sky by separating the water above from the water below. Important Terms:

<code>
  Dios
</code>

Translation Notes:

<code>
  the second day - God's work of creation was orderly, purposeful, and sequential. The things he created each day built upon and depended on the acts of the previous days.
  God spoke - God created the sky by speaking a command.
  created - God made the sky out of nothing.
  sky - This term refers to all of the space above the earth, including the air we breathe and the heavens.
</code>
