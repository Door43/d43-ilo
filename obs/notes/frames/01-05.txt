===== Pinarsuan Ti Dios Dagiti Mulmula [01-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasanna kinuna ti Dios, “Agtubo amin a klase/itsura a kaykayo ken mulmula.” Ket kasta iti napasamak. Nakita ti Dios ti inaramid na ket naayatan.

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:god|God]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:good|good]]**
==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **God said**  - God created all vegetation by speaking a command. **kinuna ti Dios**  - Pinarsuan ti Dios ti amin a ruot babaen iti mandar a sao.
  * **let the earth produce**  - This is a command that happened immediately because God spoke it. **maaddan ti daga**  - Daytoy ket mandar a panagsasao nga insigida a natungpar gupa ta sinau ti Dios.
  * **all kinds**  - The many different species, or types, of plants and trees. **amin a klase**  - Ti adu a naduma'duma nga klasen ti mulmula ken kakayo.
  * **created**  - This word is used here in the sense of making something out of nothing.**pinarsua**
  - Daytoy a sao ket nausar iti panag'aramid banbanag nga awan usaren.
  * **was good**  - This phrase is often repeated through the Creation story, and emphasizes that each stage of creation was pleasing to God and accomplished his plan and purpose.
