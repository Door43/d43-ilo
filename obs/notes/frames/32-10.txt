===== Pimmanaw N iJesus [32-10] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-10.jpg?nolink&}}Permi a mabutbuteng dagiti tattao isua imbaga da ken Jesus a pumanaw. Isua napan ni Jesus iti bangka ket nagsagana a pummanaw. Ngem nagpakaasi ti lalaki a dati nga nasaniban ti demonyo a kumuyog kenni Jesus.

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:kt:jesus]]**
  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:beg]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **people** - Some languages may prefer to say, "people from that region" or, "the Gerasene people."
  * **afraid** - It may be better to say, "afraid because of what Jesus had done."
  * **prepared** - That is, "was getting ready."
  * **begged to go along with Jesus** - That is, "begged Jesus to let him go with him" or, "earnestly asked Jesus if he could go with him."
