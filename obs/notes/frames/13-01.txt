===== Ti Bantay A Sanai [13-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Kalpasan iti panangiballasiw ti Dios dagiti Israelita manipud iti nalabbaga a baybay ket inturong na ida idiay kabakbakiran agingga iti bantay a maaw-awagan a Sinai. Daytoy met laeng diay bantay a nakakitaan ni Moses iti umap-apoy a kayo. Impatakder dagiti tattao dagiti tolda da iti poon ti bantay.

==== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita : ====

  * **burning bush**  - Before Moses returned to Egypt, God had spoken to him from a shrub that was on fire, but that was not burned up by the fire. See **[[:ilo:obs:notes:frames:09-12|[09-12]]]**.
  * **set up their tents**  - The Israelites had to travel a great distance from Egypt to the Promised Land. So they took tents with them so that they could set them up as shelters and sleep in them along the way. Some languages could translate this as, "hung their tents."
