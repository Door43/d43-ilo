===== Dagiti Timel ken dagiti Ngilaw [10-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Isunga nangted ti Dios ti didigra a** timtimel ** ken ti didigra** dagiti ngilngilaw**. Ket pinaayaban manen ti Pharaoh da Moses ken Aaron ken imbagana a no ikkatenda dagiti dididgra ket palubusanna dagiti Israelita a pumanaw idiay Egypt. Idi nagkararag ni Moses ket inikkat ti Dios dagiti ngilaw manipud idiay Egypt. Ngem** pinatangken manen ti Pharaoh ti pusona** ket saanna a pinalubusan a pumanaw dagiti tattao.

===== Pannakaipakaawat Kadagiti Sarsarita: =====

  * **nangted ti Dios iti didigra**  – This could be translated as, "God caused there to be a plague" or, "God caused a plague (of gnats) to come over the land of Egypt."
  * **ti timtimel- **  These were tiny, biting insects flying in large swarms, annoying and landing on all the people and animals of Egypt.
  * **dagiti ngilaw- **  These were much bigger flying insects that were both annoying and destructive. There were so many of these flies that they covered everything, even filling the Egyptians' houses**.**
