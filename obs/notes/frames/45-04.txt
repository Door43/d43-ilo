===== Binarsabarsakda Iti Bato Ni Stephen [45-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Idi nangngeg dagiti panguluen ti relihiyon dagiti imbaga na ket nakaungetda ket inabbunganda dagiti laplapayag da ken nagpukkawda iti napigsa. Inguyod da ni Stephen iti ruar ti ili ken inubor da.

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:ilo:obe:other:jewishleaders|Jewish leaders, religious leaders]]**
===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **covered their ears**  - This could also be translated as, "They put their hands over their ears." This was to show that they did not want to hear what Stephen said.
  * **yelled loudly**  - They were yelling in anger. Translate this in a way that communicates that they were very upset.
