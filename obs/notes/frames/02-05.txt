===== Panaglabsing [02-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Nakita ti babai diay bunga ket napintas ken naimas ti buya na. ket kayat na met nga agbalin a nasirib. Isua nangala ti bunga ket kinnan na. Ket inikkan na iti asawa na a kadwa na ket kinnan na met.

===== Important Terms: =====

  *

**[[https://door43.org/ilo/obe/kt/wise|wise]]**

==== Translation Notes: ====

  *

**wise**  - The woman wanted to have insight and understanding like the snake seemed to have, and like God has.

  *

**who was with her**  - This is important information because it shows that the man was present when the woman made the decision to eat the fruit. **[[https://door43.org/ilo/obs/notes/frames/02-04|<<]] | [[https://door43.org/ilo/obs/notes/02|Up]] | [[https://door43.org/ilo/obs/notes/frames/02-06|>>]]**
