===== Inlaban NI Peter Ni Jesus [38-15] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-15.jpg?nolink&}}

Idi tiniliwen dagiti suldado ni Jesus ket inuksot ni Peter ti kampilan na santo na tinagbat ti lapayag ti maysa nga adipen ti kangatuan a padi. Ngem kinuna ni Jesus, “Yadayom ti kampilan! Mabalin a dawatek iti Amak a mangipatulod iti anghel a mangsalaknib kanyak. Ngem masapul a tungpalek iti Amak.” Santo impasubli ni Jesus diay lapayag ti lalaki. Timmaray dagiti adalan kalpasan a natiliw ni Jesus.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As the soldiers arrested Jesus** - That is, "While the soldiers were arresting Jesus."
  * **pulled out his sword** - That is, "pulled out his sword from the place where he was carrying it."
  * **I must obey my Father** - That is, "I won't do that because I must obey my Father's will and allow myself to be arrested."
