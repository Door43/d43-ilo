===== Ti Masakit A Babai [32-13] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-13.jpg?nolink&}}

Nagsubli ni Jesus ti ballasiw iti baybay. Kalpasan a nakadanun isuna ket nagguummong dagiti adu a tattao iti aglaw-law na ket agsisinnik-sik da. Adda babae kadagidiay adu a tattao a maparparigat iti problema ti panagpadpadara nga awan sardeng na iti sanga pulo ken dua a tawen. Naboslun ti amin a kuwarta na kadagiti mangngagas tapnun maagasan ngem mas kimmarkaro pay ti kasasaad na.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pressed in on him** - That is, "pushed tightly against him" or, "crowded in tightly around him."
  * **she only got worse** - That is, "her condition only got worse" or, " her health got worse instead of better" or, "instead she became more sick."
