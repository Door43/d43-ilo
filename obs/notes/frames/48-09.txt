===== Ti Ilalabas Ti Patay [48-09] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Idi imbaun ti Dios ti maudi a didigra idiay Egipto ket inbaga na iti tunggal pamilya dagiti Israelita a mangpatay da iti maysa a naan-anay nga urbon a karnero ken iwaras da ti dara na iti hamba ti ruangan ti babbalay da. Idi makita ti Dios ti dara ket linabsan na dagitoy a babbalay ken saan na a pinatay dagiti inauna nga annak da. Daytoy a pasamak ket maaw-awagan iti Ilalabas ti Patay.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sent the last plague on Egypt**  - That is, "caused the final disaster to come on Egypt." This was the tenth plague when God caused the firstborn sons of the Egyptians to die.
  * **a perfect lamb**  - That is, "a lamb without any defect."
  * **its blood**  - That is, "the lamb's blood."
  * **door frames**  - If door frames are not known, this could be translated as, "doors."
  * **passed over**  - This could also be translated as, "passed by" or, "went by." You may want to connect how this is translated with how "Passover" is translated.
