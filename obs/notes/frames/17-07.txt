===== Ti Kari Ti Dios Kenni David [17-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Ngem imbaun ti Dios ti Mammadtu a Nathan kenni David keta awitawit na iti daytoy a ballaag, “Gapu ta sika ket mannakigubat a tao ket saan a sika ti mangpatakder iti dayta a templo para kanyak. Ti lalaki a putot mo ti mangipatakder. Ngem bendisyonan ka ti aglaplapusanan. Agturay to ti maysa kadagiti kapututam kas ari dagiti tattaok  nga awan patengga na.”  Ti Messiah ti maymaysa a kapututan ni David nga agturay iti awan patengga. Ti Mesias ket isu ti maymaysa a pinili ti Dios a mangisalakan kadagiti tattao ti lubong manipud kadagiti basbasol da.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **the prophet Nathan** – It may be more natural in some languages to say, "a prophet named Nathan."
  * **man of war** - That is, "man who fights battles." David killed many, many people in battle against the Israelites' enemies. God wasn't punishing David, but God wanted a man of peace to build the Temple where people would worship him.
  * **this Temple** - That is, "this building for worship" or, "this place of worship."
  * **from sin** - That is, "from the terrible consequences of their sin."
