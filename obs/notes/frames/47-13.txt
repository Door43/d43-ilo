===== Ti Pannakiwaras Ti Naimbag A Damag [47-13] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-47-13.jpg?nolink&}}

Iti sumaruno nga aldaw, pinalubusan dagiti panguloen ti siudad da Paul ken Silas manipud iti pagbaludan ken kiniddaw da kadakuada a pumanaw dan idiay Philippi. Binisitada ni Lydia ken dagiti daduma pay a gagayyem da iti dayta a siudad ken kalpasan na pimmanaw dan manipud iti dayta a lugar. Nagtultuloy a naisaknap ti naimbag a damag a maipapan kenni Jesus. Ken nagtultuloy a dimmakkel ti Iglesia.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the leaders of the city**  - This refers to, "the officials of the city" or, "the authorities of the city."
  * **The good news about Jesus kept spreading**  - That is, "People in more and more places were hearing the good news about Jesus."
  * **the Church kept growing**  - That is, "more and more people were becoming part of the Church" or, "more and more people were believing in Jesus."
