===== Nangisabet Ti Dios Para Kenni Elijah [19-03] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Imbaga ti Dios ken Elijah a mapan iti maysa a waig iti kabakbakiran tapno ilemmengan na ni Ahab a nagbalak a mangpatay kenkuana. Iti tunggal agsapa ken rabii ket mangip-ipan kenkuana dagiti billit iti tinapay ken karne. Birbiruken ni Ahab ken dagiti suldado na ni Elijah ngem dida mabirukan isuna. Gapo iti nakaro a tikag ket natianan metten diay waig.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **the wilderness** - This was a remote location with very few people. This may also be translated as, "desert" or, "the bush."
  * **The drought** - This could be translated as, "the lack of rain." This drought was a result of Elijah declaring that there would be no rain.
